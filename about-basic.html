Hi! Nice to meet you. I´m Valeria Davila from Peru.

I am aware of how good design can make a huge and positive impact on people's lives.

Design work is always exciting for me because I consider myself as a person who loves constantly learning new things all the time. Recently I have been taking different courses on UX/UI design and what I like the most is creating beautiful designs.
